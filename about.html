About Us

We Believe in the risen Christ - that is Jesus Christ, the Son of God that died as a sacrifice for mankind and who is the way to eternal life.

We believe the Bible is the word of God and that all scriptures (the complete Bible) is given by inspiration of God, and it's profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for insturction and righteousness.

We believe in one eternal God who exist in three distinct person- God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy spirit - who are each fully and equally God. We believe God is the totality of love and loves all mankind with unconditional love.

We strive to full our earthly purpose which is to inpact mankind and show the love of God through our deeds and to seek the reconnection of mankind to God through Jesus Christ
